Ethics Practice Programs
  • Integrity contract
    In order to strengthen the understanding of the ethics regulation and foster awareness about ethical conduct, we are implementing a ethics contract system: officer integrity agreement (officers), upholding integrity agreement (all employees), integrity agreement (contractors, part-time employees).
  • Challenge Golden Bell!
    In order to increase the understanding of Code of Conduct and denying unethical requests or bribes, we hold a quiz program, Challenge Gold Bell. It is a fun way to strengthen ethical awareness among employees.
  • Integrity forecast
    Incheon International Airport Corporation sends out messages regarding integrity especially around holidays where there are increased temptations for unethical conducts or corruption.
  • Integrity Letter
    Incheon International Airport Corporation publishes “Integrity Letter” every other month to communicate the ethics vision with everyone and to provide the most up-to-date information about integrity and ethics including a Q&A section for denying solicitations and bribes.
  • Integrity and ethics media contest
    Incheon International Airport Corporation holds media contests where any employee or airport-related workers can submit posters, videos, and other medium that promotes ethical conduct and raise awareness about integrity through diverse forms.
