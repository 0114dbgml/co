Incheon Airport Aircraft Noise Level Management

In order to minimize the effect of aircraft noise, Incheon Airport was, from the very beginning, constructed as an offshore airport. Additionally, we have developed and operate a self-monitoring system to continually reduce aircraft noise level, and based on the assessment outcome, work with airlines through the Aircraft Noise Reduction Committee Meeting to discuss and discover innovative ways to minimize noise level. We also practice countermeasures according to the legal standards and regularly open up Noise Level Forums to get comments and advice from diverse areas of expertise.
