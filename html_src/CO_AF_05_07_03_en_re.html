Airport facility interior air quality measurement

For the wellbeing of the airport users, Incheon International Airport conducts air quality measurement in the Passenger Terminal, Passenger Vehicle Waiting Area, Interior Parking Lot, among other airport facilities according to the legal level set by the “Interior Air Quality Management Act”.

We measure the fine dust level every quarter in the Passenger Terminal where there is high foot traffic, and there is a yearly maintained and recommended level measurement to provide a clean and comfortable environment for airport users.

See air quality measurement location
  • “Interior Air Quality Management Act” standards
  • Maintained level(once a year): fine dust, carbon dioxide, formaldehyde, monoxide
  • Recommended level(every 2 years): nitrogen dioxide, radon, total volatile organic compounds (Asbestos, Ozone)
