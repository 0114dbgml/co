Message from CEO

Incheon Airport is flying to new
heights as the global leading airport!

Il-Young Jung,
Incheon International Airport
Corporation CEO

Hello, , I am Il-Young Jung, the CEO of the Incheon International Airport Corporation.

Incheon International Airport, that connects the Republic of Korea with the world, has finished the long journey of the Stage-3 construction and start a 2nd flight with the grand opening of the Passenger Terminal 2, equipped with an even more convenient facility and service.

Incheon Airport has continually grown with the goal of being the hub airport of Northeast Asia and became an airport raking 7th in passenger served and 2nd in cargo processing in the world. Through putting the safety and the comforts of our customers above all, more than 50,000 airport family members have worked together to achieve the honor of being the best service airport in the world for 12 consecutive years.

We do not want to dwell in our past achievements but to taken on a new challenge for our 2nd flight. We will push forward to bring in the best service and facility for our passengers and become a futuristic smart airport in preparation for over 100 million passengers with the newest facilities and technologies. We will also rise to the center of aviation, tourism, and culture in Northeast Asia through building the AirCity.

We will go even a step further to enter the world market with Incheon Airpot's world class service and conquor new export area called the airport management system.

Please continue to show your support and encouragement so that Incheon International Airport can become a true hub of the international skies beyond northeast Asia.

Thank you.
