Environment Management Vision and Strategy

We are working to become a world leading low-emission green airport through adopting a long-term environment-friendly strategy.

  • Strategy
    • Manifestation of a low-emission, `green airport
  • Action Item
    • Reduce pollution and expand resource recycling
  • Environment Goal
    • Manifestation of a environment-friendly airport
  • Implementation
    • Manifest a healthy airport through reducing environmental pollution
    • Manifest a resource-friendly airport through expanding resource recycling
    • Manifest an environment-friendly airport management system
Organizational Structure
  • Environment Management Team
    • Environment Management Team
    • Environment Monitoring Office
  • Striving for an environment-friendly business model, Incheon International Airport Corporation has an independent Environment Management Team and also has obtained and use the Aviation/Airport Sector Environment Management System (ISO14000s).
  • Environmental agency is mad up of the Environment Management Team, which oversees the management of waste, water quality, atmospheric quality, aircraft noise level, and general environmental issues, and the Environment Monitoring Office, which conducts 24-hour monitoring around the airport.
  • Environment Monitoring Office, as a professional service agency, inspects and operates the monitoring stations, operates the environment inspection vehicle, analyses measurement data, and other work to effectively monitor the airport environment.
