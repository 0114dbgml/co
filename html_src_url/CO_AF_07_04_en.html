Pledge to Comply with Fair-trade Regulations

As employees, we understand that we must voluntarily comply with fair-trade regulation in our work, and we pledge to familiarize ourselves with faire-trade regulations and compliance programs and abide by these guidelines.

  • One. We do not conduct unfair business with resident businesses or partners by using our position of power.
  • One. We follow the procedure mandated by any related laws in any transactions and contract implementation in a transparent manner.
  • One. We will immediately report to the Compliance Office when there is a possibility that we might violate the fair-trade regulation.

As such, we pledge to do our utmost in our given position to ensure that our public corporation becomes world first-class corporation managed according to legal guidance.
