Field Trip Preview

Tours take place through the Transit Center and Passenger Terminals,
and it takes about an hour on foot.

  1. step 01
    Terminal Floor 3 Exit 1
    Explanation of the history of Incheon Airport,
    current issues, and facilities
  2. step 02
    Departure Area
    Explanation about check-in, security check,
    and other departure procedure
  3. step 03
    Terminal Floor 4 Korean Cultural Hall
    See traditional Korean family home and aircraft parking ramp
  4. step 04
    Terminal Floor 1, Millennium Hall
    Explanation about Millennium Hall
  5. step 05
    Transit Center Basement 1
    Explanation about Airport Train and
    general information about the Transit Center
  6. step 06
    Floor 1 Exit 14
    End of tour

유의사항

  • The above course might change depending on the airport management.
  • All tours are through the public accessible areas, not restricted areas.
  • Airport Field Trip does not include career, aircraft, or reporting experience activities.
