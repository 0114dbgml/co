Incheon Airport Observation Deck

Viewing Program Information

Incheon Airport Observation Deck (Ohsung Mt. Observation Deck), located 51.5 m above sea level, is the best place to see the whole airport at once, and you can see aircrafts taking off and landing on the runway.
You can follow the path from the Observation Deck parking lot to the Observation Deck while enjoying the beauty of nature and get a unique view of the airport.

  • Operating hours :
    • Operating hours: Winter (Dec-Feb), Other seasons (Mar-Nov) : 10:00-17:00 (year-round)
    • In the case of snow or heavy rain, call ahead to check operating hours.
  • Request Period : At least a day in advance.
  • Reservation and Inquiries : Incheon Airport Observation Deck 032-751-2117
Location and Getting Here
  • Location : Incheon Joong-gu Airport Road 279 (Nambuk-dong)  
    (For Navigation, search: Incheon Airport Observation Deck) 
  • Public Transportation : Bus 306 (Passenger Terminal Floor 3 No. 2, Stop 13)
