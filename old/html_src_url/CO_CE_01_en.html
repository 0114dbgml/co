Volunteering at Incheon International Airport

This is an event that young adults and older adults can take part in in order to contribute to friendly airport management and healthy local community development. Anyone interested in volunteering can receive a notice through the website and can participate after applying and being selected as a part of the Volunteer Team.

Volunteer Team(Incheon Airport Ambassador)

1 year volunteer program for professionals with foreign-language skills and service mindset.

Young Adult Volunteer Group(Incheon Airport Junior Ambassador)

Service project for passionate and service-minded young adults during school vacations.

Application information
  • Those interested in joining the Volunteer Team should check for recruitment posts and submit an application.
  • All applicants are considered by the HR department and selected by the Volunteer Team Management Plan.
Volunteer Code of Conduct
  1. 1 As a member of the Incheon International Airport’s representative Volunteer Team, please familiarize yourself with Volunteer Team’ vision, purpose, and goal and provide a friendly and proactive service to all airport users.
  2. 2 Be aware that all activities are to help the Incheon International Airport customers and work in a customer-centered manner.
  3. 3 Arrive ready for work 15 minutes before the start of your shift.
  4. 4 Please wear the designated Volunteer Team uniform, nametag, and bag.
